Team Description

Engineering Team

If you are looking for opportunities to apply your programming skills to create to real-world impact — Join us @ the engineering team!
Responsibilities
  • Construct and maintain the front-end or back-end of our application
  • Work with other teams to test new features and incorporate feedbacks into each iteration
Preferred qualifications
One of the following:
  • Backend Focus: strong foundation on objected-oriented languages (eg. C++/Java) or script languages (eg. Python)
  • Algorithm Focus: experience with machine learning/NLP, or alternative technologies relevant to chatbot development
  • Frontend Focus: knowledge of at least one frontend framework (eg.React, Angular,) or experience with Flutter

Design Team

If you have a user-centered mindset and interested in brainstorming and designing product features — Join us @ the UX design team!
Responsibilities
  • Research and design the user flow & interface
  • Work closely with content and front-end developers to design and implement features
  • Facilitate user interviews and iterate the designs through user feedback
Preferred qualifications
  • Knowledge in human-centered design
  • Experience with Figma and Adobe design tools
  • Experience with HTML/CSS is a plus

Growth & Marketing Team

If you are interested in entrepreneurship, product growth, marketing, and want to build a startup product and participate in the 0 to 1 user accumulation stage — Join us @ the growth and marketing team!
Responsibilities
  • Define the product growth plan
  • Explore new ways to engage and identify users
  • Build and manage our social media profiles and presence
  • Run regular digital and offline marketing campaigns and track the success

Content Team

If you are interested in psychology, mental health, helping others with peer pressure, and enjoy seeing users directly benefiting from your work — Join us @ the content team!
Responsibilities
  • Design and test new self-care exercises
  • Collaborate with designers to digitalize them
  • Conduct user interviews to ensure the quality of the exercise and other features
