Team Description

Engineering Team

We leverage state-of-the-art machine learning models to explore sentiments behind words. If you are looking for opportunities to apply your engineering skills to create to real-world impact — Join us @ the engineering team!
Responsibilities
  • Conduct experiments to improve NLP model performance
  • Implement machine learning algorithms into mobile apps
  • Work with other teams to test new features and incorporate feedbacks into each iteration
Software Engineer Requirements
  • Minumum: Strong foundation in data structure and algorithm. Basic knowledge of JavaScript/Typescript.
  • Preferred: Experience in Native iOS/Android Development and/or cross-platform frameworks such as React Native and Flutter.
Machine Learning Engineer Requirements
  • Minumum: A solid understanding in machine learning and statistical inference.
  • Preferred: Have taken CS189/STAT154 or other equivalent ML courses. Project or research experience in Natural Language Processing or general machine learning.

Design Team

If you have a user-centered mindset and interested in brainstorming and designing product features — Join us @ the UX design team!
Responsibilities
  • Research and design the user flow & interface
  • Work closely with content and front-end developers to design and implement features
  • Facilitate user interviews and iterate the designs through user feedback
Preferred qualifications
  • Knowledge in human-centered design
  • Experience with Figma and Adobe design tools
  • Experience with HTML/CSS is a plus

Product Growth Team

If you are interested in entrepreneurship, product growth, marketing, and want to build a startup product and participate in the 0 to 1 user accumulation stage — Join us @ the product growth and marketing team!
Requirements
Have a familiarity or experitise in at least one of the following fields:
  • Product: Develop product requirement and product growth plan, ship the product, track the success, and drive iterations
  • Marketing: Build and manage social media platforms & explore new ways to identify and engage users & run marketing campaigns
  • Outreach:Reach out to potential partnership organizations, advisors, investors, and clients
  • Business: Conduct market research & develop pitch deck and proposals

Content Team

If you are interested in psychology, mental health, helping others with peer pressure, and enjoy seeing users directly benefiting from your work — Join us @ the content team!
Responsibilities
  • Conduct professional research on mental healthcare topics and develop exercise scopes.
  • Design fully developed exercises based upon secondary research and peer reviews
  • Create personalized features to enhance the interactivity among users and Echo
  • Collaborate with Design Team to digitalize developed exercises
