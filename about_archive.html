Why care about mental health

Self-care has become a buzzword as people’s awareness of mental health grows. More than 60 percent of college students said they had experienced “overwhelming anxiety,” and over 40 percent said they felt so depressed that they had difficulty functioning.

The challenge

The increase in attention of day-to-day mental health has given rise to a boom of meditation apps, online counseling, and even wellness-themed youtube channels. Yet the amount of options is overwhelming and obstructive to self-care.

Why Re:treat

Re:treat not only saves your time and energy for finding a suitable mental exercise, but also validates the exercise with research evidence and connects you with professional counselors and the community.

Our Solution

Self-care Exercise

With a few self-introspective questions and backed by relevant research, Re:treat will recommend and guide you through suitable self-care exercises.

Professional Help

Replying your anonymous posts, invited psychology counselors will give out their professional suggestions that would help you acquire a healthier emotional state.

The Community

Sharing your moments of happiness or your emotional distress, your community on Re:treat will always be supportive and encouraging each other.
