Assuage your emotional distress instantly by labeling your short-term daily hassles. We will generate your personalized 5-to-10-minute self-care mental exercises and connect you to the community anonymously.

Why care about mental health

Self-care has become a buzzword as people’s awareness of mental health grows. More than 60 percent of college students said they had experienced “overwhelming anxiety,” and over 40 percent said they felt so depressed that they had difficulty functioning.

The challenge

The increase in attention of day-to-day mental health has given rise to a boom of meditation apps, online counseling, and even wellness-themed youtube channels. Yet the amount of options is overwhelming and obstructive to self-care.

Why Re:treat

Re:treat not only saves your time and energy for finding a suitable mental exercise, but also validates the exercise with research evidence and introduces you to the community.

Our Solution

Instant

Labeling your emotional distress easily with only a few self-introspective questions, Re:treat helps you to diagnose your daily mental hassles promptly at your fingertip.

Personalized

Based on your emotion, Re:treat saves your time searching and comparing the best suitable mental exercises and sifts out the relevant ones that would help you the most.

Effective

Backed by psychology and mental health research, Re:treat's short yet refined exercise recommendations are effective and will help you to acquire a healthier mental state.
